# Belief Evolution — One Belief Across Six Documents

This file traces a single belief from the moment it was first seeded
through six document reads. It shows how the statement sharpens, how
confidence moves, and what caused each update.

---

## The Belief Being Tracked

**NC-02 — Target Credibility**

*The belief that annual targets are consistently set and then revised
downward, with misses absorbed as new baselines rather than triggering
recovery plans.*

---

## Cycle 1 — Annual Report (Year 1)

**What the document contained:**
Full-year results. Revenue 4% below the target set at the start of
the year. The year-end commentary described the shortfall as driven
by "a softer external environment in the second half." Next year's
guidance was set 2% below the original Year 1 target — described as
"a prudent and grounded starting point."

**What was extracted:**
One miss, attributed externally. One guidance point set below the
prior target with no recovery language.

**Decision:** SEEDED
This is the first document. One miss, one guidance reset. Not enough
for a belief about a pattern. But the combination — miss attributed
externally, guidance reset rather than recovered — is specific enough
to track.

**Belief statement at Cycle 1:**
"Annual revenue targets have been missed and the guidance for the
following year has been set below the original target without a
recovery path. One observation."
Confidence: 0.20 | Direction: Unclear | Evidence: 1

---

## Cycle 2 — Mid-Year Review (Year 2)

**What the document contained:**
First-half results. 3% below the half-year plan. Full-year guidance
revised down from the original annual target. Attributed to "demand
timing" expected to improve in H2.

**What was extracted:**
Within-year guidance revision. Same external attribution framing as
Year 1. The H2 recovery claim is a forward commitment — trackable.

**Decision:** CONFIRMED
Pattern beginning to emerge. Within-year revision plus H2 recovery
claim that can be closed at year end.

**Belief statement at Cycle 2:**
"Annual targets have been revised downward mid-year in both observed
years, attributed to external timing. Full-year recovery is being
committed to — this is a testable claim."
Confidence: 0.28 | Direction: Deteriorating | Evidence: 2

---

## Cycle 3 — Annual Report (Year 2)

**What the document contained:**
Full-year results. Revenue 6% below the revised mid-year target.
The H2 recovery committed to in Cycle 2 did not materialize. Year 3
guidance set 4% below the original Year 2 target. Commentary
described Year 3 as "a reset year for rebuilding momentum."

**What was extracted:**
The H2 recovery commitment from Cycle 2 was not met.
Second consecutive year of miss-then-reset.
"Reset year" language introduces a new framing — the miss is now
being absorbed as intentional.

**Decision:** CONFIRMED — significant
The H2 recovery commitment created a falsifiable test. Year 2 results
failed it. The pattern — miss, attribute, reset guidance downward,
describe as deliberate — is now visible across two full years.

**Belief statement at Cycle 3:**
"Annual targets are consistently missed and the guidance for the
following year is set below the prior original target. Commitments
to recovery made mid-year have not been met. The organization absorbs
misses as new baselines rather than driving recovery."
Confidence: 0.52 | Direction: Deteriorating | Evidence: 3

---

## Cycle 4 — Strategy Presentation (Year 3)

**What the document contained:**
A multi-year strategic roadmap. Year 3 commitments described with
specific numeric targets. Three-year aspiration stated in revenue
and margin terms.

**What was extracted:**
New specific commitments introduced. These are now trackable.
No reference to Year 1 or Year 2 targets — the prior misses are
not acknowledged in the framing of Year 3 ambitions.

**Decision:** NEUTRAL — watching
New commitments have been made. They will either be met or missed.
The belief does not update here — there is nothing to confirm or
contradict yet. The new targets are filed in factual understanding
as a commitment record.

**Belief statement at Cycle 4:** UNCHANGED
Confidence: 0.52 | Direction: Deteriorating | Evidence: 3

---

## Cycle 5 — Mid-Year Review (Year 3)

**What the document contained:**
First-half results below the half-year plan. The specific numeric
targets from the Cycle 4 strategy presentation have been replaced
in the commentary with directional language — "building toward
medium-term ambitions." No specific figure for the year-end target
is restated.

**What was extracted:**
Numeric commitments from Cycle 4 have been softened to directional
language within one planning cycle. This is a new layer to the
pattern — not just missing targets but withdrawing them before the
period they cover has ended.

**Decision:** CONFIRMED — and belief sharpened
This adds a new element: targets are not only missed ex-post but
withdrawn mid-cycle. The belief now covers both the miss-and-reset
pattern and the active softening of forward commitments under pressure.

**Belief statement at Cycle 5:**
"Annual targets are consistently missed and reset downward. Specific
forward commitments made in strategy documents are softened to
directional language within the same planning cycle, before the
period they cover has ended. The organization withdraws commitments
under pressure rather than holding and recovering them."
Confidence: 0.69 | Direction: Deteriorating | Evidence: 5

---

## Cycle 6 — Annual Report (Year 3)

**What the document contained:**
Full-year results. Revenue 5% below the original Year 3 target
(which had been replaced with directional language in Cycle 5).
Year 4 guidance set at a level 3% below Year 3's original target.
Commentary described Year 4 as "a year of disciplined execution."

**What was extracted:**
Third consecutive annual miss-and-reset. The pattern is consistent
across three full years and six documents.

**Decision:** CONFIRMED
The pattern has now repeated across a full three-year cycle with
consistent structure: target → miss → external attribution → guidance
reset below prior target → describe as deliberate.

**Belief statement at Cycle 6:** (final for this illustration)
"Annual targets have been missed and reset downward for three
consecutive years. Commitments to recovery have not been met.
Specific numeric forward targets are softened to directional language
under pressure before the period they cover ends. The guidance process
appears to manage expectations rather than reflect genuine operational
ambitions."
Confidence: 0.81 | Direction: Deteriorating | Evidence: 6

---

## What This Evolution Shows

1. **Silence at first** is not failure — Cycle 1 produced a seed,
   not a belief. The system waited.

2. **Each update sharpened the statement** — the belief in Cycle 6
   is more precise and more useful than the one in Cycle 3, because
   new evidence revealed a new dimension (mid-cycle withdrawal of
   commitments) that wasn't visible earlier.

3. **Falsifiable tests were built in** — the H2 recovery commitment
   in Cycle 2 was explicitly noted as a test. When Cycle 3 showed
   it failed, the confidence rose meaningfully.

4. **Neutral cycles are valid** — Cycle 4 produced no update because
   new commitments were made but not yet tested. The system stayed
   silent until evidence arrived.

5. **The belief statement is an interpretation, not a summary** —
   it names what the pattern implies, not just what happened.
